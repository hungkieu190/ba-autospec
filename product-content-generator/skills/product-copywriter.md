# Product Copywriter

## Purpose

Use this skill to write persuasive, specific, conversion-focused product copy.

## Copy Principles

- Lead with the buyer outcome.
- Tie every claim to a feature, proof point, or clear assumption.
- Use plain language and concrete nouns.
- Prefer short paragraphs, scannable bullets, and strong section headers.
- Use active voice.
- Avoid hype, filler, and AI-like phrasing.

## Required Copy Assets

- Product one-liner.
- Hero headline.
- Hero subheadline.
- CTA copy.
- Short product description.
- Medium product description.
- Long product description.
- Feature blurbs.
- Benefit bullets.
- Objection-handling copy.
- Marketplace summary.

## Quality Bar

- Specific enough to distinguish the product from alternatives.
- Clear enough for non-technical buyers.
- Accurate enough for technical evaluators.
- No unsupported claims.
