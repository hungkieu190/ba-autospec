# SEO Keyword Research

## Purpose

Use this skill to create an SEO keyword strategy for product pages, landing pages, comparisons, FAQs, and blog content.

## Source Basis

Consolidated from seo-specialist, content-marketer, market-researcher, and search-specialist patterns.

## Keyword Groups

- Primary product keyword.
- Commercial keywords.
- Transactional keywords.
- Comparison keywords.
- Alternative keywords.
- Integration keywords.
- Problem-aware keywords.
- Tutorial keywords.
- FAQ keywords.

## Rules

- Do not invent search volume unless a source is provided.
- Use High/Medium/Low potential when exact data is unavailable.
- Prioritize buyer-intent keywords over generic high-volume terms.
- Map every important keyword to a page section or content asset.
- Include schema opportunities: Product, FAQPage, HowTo, BreadcrumbList, Review only when evidence exists.
