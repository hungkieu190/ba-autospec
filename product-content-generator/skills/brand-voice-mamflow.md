# Brand Voice: MamFlow

## Purpose

Use this skill to keep product content consistent with MamFlow/ThimPress-style WordPress product marketing while borrowing the structure and clarity documented in `product-content-generator/woocommerce-style-reference.md`.

## Voice Attributes

- Practical.
- Clear.
- Confident.
- Builder-friendly.
- Merchant-focused.
- Helpful without hype.
- Technical when necessary, but never obscure.

## Tone Rules

- Write like a product team that understands WordPress businesses.
- Use concise Vietnamese by default.
- Keep specialist terms in English when they are clearer: subscription, checkout, membership, access rule, renewal, webhook, shortcode, block, REST API, HPOS, Cart and Checkout Blocks.
- Avoid over-polished marketing fluff.
- Prefer concrete outcomes: sell recurring access, reduce manual setup, protect premium content, simplify checkout, lower support burden.

## WooCommerce-Inspired Style Rules

- Start with a concise product promise.
- Use feature bullets early.
- Use plain benefit-led sections.
- Include practical setup/getting-started steps.
- Include compatibility, support, documentation, and guarantee-style modules when accurate.
- Use FAQ to address real buyer objections.
- Mention ratings/installs/version only when verified.

## Banned Patterns

- Revolutionary solution.
- Seamless experience without explanation.
- Robust platform without proof.
- Unlock the power of.
- Supercharge your business.
- Any claim that implies verified traction without evidence.
