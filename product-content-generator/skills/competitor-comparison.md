# Competitor Comparison

## Purpose

Use this skill to create fair competitor comparisons, alternative pages, and feature comparison sections.

## Source Basis

Consolidated from competitive-analyst, market-researcher, content-marketer, and sales-engineer patterns.

## Required Comparison Areas

- Positioning.
- Target user.
- Pricing model.
- Core features.
- Integrations.
- UX/admin workflow.
- Support/docs.
- Strengths.
- Weaknesses.
- Best fit.

## Rules

- Never invent competitors.
- Never fabricate competitor weaknesses.
- Use public sources or mark as `Cần validate`.
- Keep tone fair and buyer-helpful, not hostile.
- For comparison/alternative content, explain who each product is best for.
