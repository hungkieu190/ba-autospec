## Required Style Reference

Use WooCommerce product pages, especially WooCommerce Subscriptions, as the structural and tonal reference:

- Marketplace-style product header.
- Product promise and visual hero.
- Pricing/CTA block.
- Trust/support/compatibility modules.
- Feature bullets near the top.
- Benefit-led feature sections.
- Getting started steps.
- FAQ.
- Related/comparison sections when relevant.
