# Customer Persona

## Purpose

Use this skill to define who the product page is speaking to and what each buyer/user needs to believe before converting.

## Persona Inputs

- Primary buyer.
- End user.
- Technical evaluator.
- Business stakeholder.
- Support/admin operator.
- Existing platform user.

## Persona Framework

For each persona define:

| Field | Guidance |
| --- | --- |
| Role | Job title or site role |
| Context | Where and why they need the product |
| Pain | What is broken today |
| Desired outcome | What success looks like |
| Objections | Why they may not buy |
| Proof needed | What evidence reduces doubt |
| Best message | What copy angle will resonate |

## Rules

- Separate buyer from user when they differ.
- For WordPress/WooCommerce products, include store owner/admin and developer personas when relevant.
- Persona claims must come from input, research, reviews, or clearly marked assumptions.
- Use personas to guide copy hierarchy, FAQ, and CTAs.
