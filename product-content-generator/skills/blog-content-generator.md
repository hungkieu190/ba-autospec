# Blog Content Generator

## Purpose

Use this skill to create SEO-driven blog ideas and article drafts that support product discovery and conversion.

## Blog Content Types

- Tutorial articles.
- Use case articles.
- Comparison articles.
- Alternative articles.
- Problem-solution articles.
- Integration guides.
- Launch announcement.
- Product education articles.

## Blog Brief Format

| Topic | Intent | Target Persona | Funnel Stage | Product Angle | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Article Rules

- Match search intent before promoting the product.
- Use the product naturally as a solution, not forced advertising.
- Include internal links to product page, docs, FAQ, and comparison pages.
- Include examples relevant to WordPress/WooCommerce/LMS store owners when applicable.
- Mark research gaps instead of inventing data.

## Required Output

- At least 20 blog/content ideas.
- 3 detailed article briefs.
- 1 launch announcement draft.
- Internal linking plan.
