## Product Descriptions

### Short Version

Sell LearnPress membership plans through WooCommerce checkout and manage member access inside LearnPress.

### Medium Version

Memberships & Subscriptions Add-on for LearnPress helps LMS site owners sell membership plans instead of only individual courses. Use WooCommerce checkout, payment gateways, coupons, tax, invoices and Woo Subscriptions while keeping the membership dashboard and plan access experience inside LearnPress.

### Long Version

Memberships & Subscriptions Add-on for LearnPress turns your LearnPress site into a plan-based membership business. Instead of selling every course one by one, you can offer membership plans and let customers purchase through WooCommerce checkout. This gives your site access to WooCommerce gateways, coupons, tax handling, invoices, order management and subscription billing through Woo Subscriptions. Students can buy a plan, land on their membership dashboard, and view their membership status from LearnPress profile and Woo account areas. Restrict Content by plan is planned as the next phase to complete the all-in-one membership workflow for LearnPress.
