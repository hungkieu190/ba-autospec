## Tóm Tắt

**LP Membership v4.1 thắng ở:**
- ✅ Mọi tính năng LearnPress integration (5 items unique)
- ✅ Dual checkout (LP + Woo)
- ✅ Plan-centric admin UI
- ✅ Course-plan mapping

**LP Membership v4.1 thiếu so với competitors:**
- ❌ Drip/delayed content (future)
- ❌ AND logic multi-plan (future)
- ❌ Member discounts (out of scope)
- ❌ Import/export rules (future)
